### 10-Day Itinerary in Hyderabad

**Day 1: Arrival and Explore the Old City**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit **Charminar**, the iconic symbol of Hyderabad.
  - Explore **Laad Bazaar** for bangles and traditional jewelry.
- **Lunch:** Enjoy authentic **Hyderabadi Biryani** at **Paradise**.
- **Afternoon:** Visit **Macca Masjid** and stroll around the historic area.
- **Dinner:** Dine at **Bawarchi** for more delicious Biryani.

---

**Day 2: Forts and Tombs**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit **Golconda Fort** and enjoy panoramic views.
- **Lunch:** Eat at **KFC** (for a quick meal) or a local eatery nearby.
- **Afternoon:** Head to **Qutb Shahi Tombs** to appreciate Indo-Islamic architecture.
- **Dinner:** Try local cuisine at **Shah Ghouse Cafe**.

---

**Day 3: Museums and Culture**
- **Morning:**
  - Explore the **Salar Jung Museum**, rich in artifacts.
- **Lunch:** Lunch at **Chutneys** known for its South Indian delicacies.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the **Nizam's Museum** to learn about their opulent lifestyle.
- **Dinner:** Dine at **The Fisherman's Wharf** for a mix of coastal and continental cuisines.

---

**Day 4: Nature and Parks**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit **NTR Gardens** for a morning stroll and relaxation.
- **Lunch:** Have a picnic or casual meal in the gardens.
- **Afternoon:** Head to **KBR National Park** for trekking and wildlife spotting.
- **Dinner:** Enjoy dinner at **Cafe Bahar**, another Biryani hotspot.

---

**Day 5: Adventure and Water Sports**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit **Hussain Sagar Lake**; engage in boating and water sports.
- **Lunch:** Eat at **Eat India Company** for a fusion experience.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the **Buddha Statue** and enjoy the scenic views.
- **Dinner:** Experience lakeside dining at **Waterfront Restaurant**.

---

**Day 6: Film City and Shopping**
- **Morning:**
  - Explore **Ramoji Film City**. Take guided tours and enjoy live shows.
- **Lunch:** Have lunch at the film city’s eateries.
- **Afternoon:** Shop for local handicrafts at **Shilparamam**.
- **Dinner:** Taste street food at **Jubilee Hills** area.

---

**Day 7: Historical Insights**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit the **Birla Mandir**, a beautiful temple built from white marble.
- **Lunch:** Eat at **Eagle Boys Pizza** if you are in the mood for pizza.
- **Afternoon:** Visit **Falaknuma Palace** for a guided tour.
- **Dinner:** Enjoy royal dining at **Falaknuma Palace** (reservation needed).

---

**Day 8: Explore Hidden Gems**
- **Morning:**
  - Visit **Paigah Tombs** and appreciate their architecture.
- **Lunch:** Try the local thali at **Narayana's Cafe**.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the serene **Durgam Cheruvu**, also known as the secret lake.
- **Dinner:** Dine at **The Nishat**, renowned for its ambience.

---

**Day 9: Day Out Close by Hyderbad**
- **Day Trip:**
  - Take a trip to **Ananthagiri Hills** for hiking and refreshing views.
- **Lunch:** Pack a picnic lunch or try local eateries around the area.
- **Evening:** Visit **Bhagyanagar Lake** to relax.
- **Dinner:** Return to Hyderabad and enjoy dinner at **Gulshan-e-Iqbal**.

---

**Day 10: Leisure and Departure**
- **Morning:**
  - Enjoy a leisurely morning at **Nehru Zoological Park**.
- **Lunch:** Quick bite at **Subway** or nearby fast food.
- **Afternoon:** Last-minute shopping at **Hyderabad Central**.
- **Departure:** Head to the airport for your flight back.

This itinerary will allow you to explore the rich cultural heritage, enjoy the vibrant local cuisine, and experience the modern amenities of Hyderabad! Enjoy your trip!
