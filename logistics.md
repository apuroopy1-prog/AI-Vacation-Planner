Here are three suitable hotel options in Hyderabad along with the typical flight costs from Atlanta:

### Hotel Options:
1. **Taj Krishna Hyderabad**
   - **Location**: Banjara Hills
   - **Price**: Approximately $130 per night
   - **Features**: Luxurious accommodations, outdoor pool, spa, and multiple dining options. This hotel is close to key attractions like Hussain Sagar Lake and Banjara Hills.

2. **Hyderabad Marriott Hotel & Convention Centre**
   - **Location**: Tank Bund Road
   - **Price**: Approximately $150 per night
   - **Features**: Modern hotel with waterfront views, fitness center, outdoor pool, and restaurants. It's conveniently located near the Salar Jung Museum and NTR Gardens.

3. **ITC Kakatiya, a Luxury Collection Hotel**
   - **Location**: Begumpet
   - **Price**: Approximately $170 per night
   - **Features**: Elegant rooms, spa services, and fine dining. Close to cultural sites like the Charminar and Qutb Shahi Tombs.

### Typical Flight Costs from Atlanta to Hyderabad:
- **Round-Trip Flight Cost**: Approximately $1,000 - $1,500
  - **Airlines**: Major carriers such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, and Air India offer flights with one or more layovers.
  - **Duration**: Flight time is around 18-20 hours including layover time.
